About

About Me

I am currently serving as an Associate Professor in the Electrical Cluster at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun, with over 15 years of experience in teaching and research. My academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from G. B. University, India, with a research focus on the performance improvement of hybrid power generation systems. I also hold an M.Tech (Hons.) in Instrumentation and Control from Aligarh Muslim University and a B.Tech in Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering. I have had the privilege of working as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand, where I contributed to a project on game puzzles and optimization techniques for enhancing photovoltaic systems under shading conditions. My research interests lie in renewable energy systems, photovoltaic optimization, and sustainable energy solutions.
